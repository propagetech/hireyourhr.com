ABOUT US

Our Story

Hire Your HR is a Human Capital Management Firm working towards excellence in the Consulting space.

With around 20 years of strong experience, Hire Your HR provides back-office support to small businesses in USA.

Our mission is to facilitate organisations with much needed HR and operational support which can address the pain areas and enable them to focus on the revenue generating activities.

Our Vision

Our vision is to provide a needed service which addresses specific pain areas of the businesses with utmost confidentiality and reliability

We firmly believe that our customers are the reason for our existence, and greatly respect the trust that they place in us. We integrate honesty, integrity and business ethics into all aspects of our business functioning.

Our mission is to build long term relationships with our customers. We strive towards delighting our customers at every opportunity through exceptional customer service.

OUR SERVICES

We offer a full range of tailor-made Human Resource and Operational services. Our involvement can start from HR Gap analysis, forming HR strategy, Drafting, revising contracts, handbooks, policies, onboarding, off boarding, performance evaluation to areas including managing exits, contract management, immigration support and compliance, timesheets, invoices etc.

WHY WE & HOW WE WORK

Why we

Our team consists of professionally qualified, long experience members in the field of Human Resources, law and Immigration.

The team has diverse knowledge and know of the real-life business complexities which any small business house may face due to current dynamic business environment. Therefore, our services are very agile and tested to suit your kind of business environment challenges.

We work with our clients as partners and with each other as a team. Our advice and solutions are shaped by each client’s unique needs and business context and are designed to ensure that clients get the best return on their HR spend.
