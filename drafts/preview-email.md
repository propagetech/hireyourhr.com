## Why the new site is a step up from the old one

The previous site was a single, long page exported from a website builder. It was slow, hard to navigate, and largely invisible to Google. The new Hire Your HR site was rebuilt from the ground up as a proper professional-firm website:

- **Clear structure:** the old site was one long page; the new one has dedicated pages (Home, About, Services, Why us, FAQ and Contact), so visitors find what they need fast.
- **Services made scannable:** instead of a flat list, your full range is grouped into four areas (HR strategy and advisory, Policies and documentation, Compliance and immigration, and Back-office and operations), each with a plain "what you get" list.
- **Better Google visibility:** unique titles and descriptions, a sitemap, and schema markup, with the FAQ eligible for Google rich results. The old site had almost none of this.
- **Faster:** no heavy builder code, with self-hosted fonts and optimised images, so pages load quickly and do not jump around.
- **Works on every device:** fully responsive, tested on phone, tablet and desktop.
- **Accessible to everyone:** built to WCAG 2.1 AA, with colour contrast checked by an automated tool on every page.
- **A stronger look and feel:** a warm, credible design with colours drawn from your logo and custom illustrations, in place of the old generic template.
- **Cleaner, safer contact:** email led, with nothing stored on the site, so visitor data stays private.

In short: easier for small-business owners to use, more likely to be found on Google, faster, and a much stronger reflection of Hire Your HR as an experienced, dependable HR partner.
