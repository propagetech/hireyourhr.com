Frequently asked questions

Clear answers on what we do, who we help, how an engagement works, and how to get started with Hire Your HR.

What does Hire Your HR do?

Hire Your HR is a Human Capital Management firm that provides back-office HR and operational support to small businesses in the USA. With around 20 years of experience, we cover HR strategy and advisory, policies and documentation, compliance and immigration, and back-office operations, so owners can focus on growing the business.

Who do you work with?

We work with small businesses, founders and operations or finance leaders in the USA who need reliable HR and back-office support without building a full in-house HR team. We also support businesses hiring across borders who need immigration support and compliance.

Do you replace our in-house HR team?

Not necessarily. Many clients have no in-house HR and use us as their HR function; others have a small team and use us to add capacity and specialist HR, law and immigration expertise. We shape the engagement around what you already have.

What HR and operations services do you offer?

Our services are grouped into four areas: HR strategy and advisory (gap analysis, HR strategy, performance evaluation); policies and documentation (contracts, handbooks, policies, onboarding, off-boarding, exits); compliance and immigration (immigration support, compliance, contract management); and back-office and operations (timesheets, invoices and day-to-day support).

Can you help with immigration support and compliance?

Yes. Our team is qualified in HR, law and immigration, so we help small businesses hire compliantly, including immigration support for onboarding global talent and keeping records audit-ready. We keep the detail general until we understand your specific situation.

How does an engagement work?

We start by understanding your business and pain areas, then tailor agile, tested services to your scope and goals. Our experienced team delivers to a consistent standard and stays a steady extension of your team, adjusting as you grow. Engagements can be a one-off project or ongoing support.

How is pricing structured?

Pricing depends on the scope and whether you need a one-off project or ongoing support, which we agree up front. Our partnership model is focused on giving you the best return on your HR spend, so the engagement is sized to the value it delivers. Email us and we will scope it with you.

How do we get started?

Email us at info@hireyourhr.com with a brief note about your business and what you need. We will respond promptly to set up a conversation. There is no form on this website and nothing is stored here.
