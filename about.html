Hire Your HR is a Human Capital Management firm built to take the back-office HR and operational burden off small businesses, so owners can focus on growth.

Our story

A Human Capital Management firm for small business

Hire Your HR is a Human Capital Management firm working towards excellence in the consulting space. With around 20 years of strong experience, we provide back-office HR and operational support to small businesses in the USA.

Our mission is to facilitate organisations with the HR and operational support they need: support that addresses their pain areas and frees them to focus on the revenue-generating work that matters.

We believe small businesses deserve the same quality of HR thinking as larger ones, without the cost and overhead of building a full in-house team.

Our vision

A dependable partner you can trust

Our vision is to provide a genuinely needed service that addresses the specific pain areas of each business, with utmost confidentiality and reliability.

Our customers are the reason we exist, and we deeply respect the trust they place in us. We bring honesty, integrity and business ethics to every part of how we work, and we aim to build long-term relationships rather than one-off transactions.

Our team is made up of professionally qualified, long-experience members in Human Resources, law and immigration. That mix means we understand the real-world complexities a small business faces, and our services are agile and tested to suit your environment.
